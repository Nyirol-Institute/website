Empowering South Sudanese Youth Through Technology

Nyirol Institute of Innovation and Technology provides education and training to help youth break the cycle of poverty and build careers in the tech industry.

Our Programs Support Our Mission

Our Mission

Education

We provide quality technology education to South Sudanese youth in Nyirol County, equipping them with digital literacy and advanced tech skills.

Innovation

We foster innovation and creativity through hands-on projects and real-world problem-solving using sustainable technology solutions.

Opportunity

We create pathways to employment and higher education through partnerships with organizations and universities around the world.

Digital Literacy

Students begin with a 3-month digital literacy program covering computer basics, internet skills, office applications, and introduction to programming concepts. This foundation prepares them for more advanced technology training.

Our solar-powered computer labs ensure consistent access to technology even in areas with limited electricity.

Advanced Tech Specialization

Based on performance and interest, students advance to a 6-month specialization in one of the following areas:

  • Graphic Design
  • UX/UI Design
  • Frontend Development
  • Backend Development
  • Data Science
  • Data Analytics
  • Animation

Each specialization includes hands-on projects and portfolio development.

Job Placement & Internships

After completing their specialization, students are connected with our partner organizations for internships and job opportunities. Our career services team provides resume building, interview preparation, and job search assistance.

University Pathway

Students interested in pursuing higher education can join our 8-month university pathway program, which prepares them for online or in-person degree programs at our partner universities. This includes academic preparation, application assistance, and scholarship opportunities.
