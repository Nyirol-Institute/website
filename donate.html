Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, Nyirol Institute is a registered non-profit organization, and all donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You will receive a receipt for your donation that can be used for tax purposes.

How is my donation used?

Your donation directly supports our educational programs, including digital literacy training, tech specialization courses, job placement services, and university pathway preparation. Funds are also used to maintain our solar-powered facilities and provide necessary technology resources for our students.

Can I specify how my donation is used?

Yes, you can designate your donation for a specific program or purpose. Please include this information in the comments section of the donation form or contact us directly to discuss your preferences.

How can I verify that my donation is making an impact?

We provide regular updates to our donors through newsletters, impact reports, and personal stories from our students. You can also request specific information about how your donation has been used and the impact it has created.

Can I visit the institute to see my donation at work?

Yes, we welcome donors to visit our campus in Nyirol County. Please contact us in advance to arrange a visit, and we would be happy to show you our facilities and introduce you to our students and staff.
