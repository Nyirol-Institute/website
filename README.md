# Nyirol Institute of Innovation and Technology

This is the official website for the Nyirol Institute of Innovation and Technology, a non-profit higher educational organization helping South Sudanese youth in Nyirol County break into the tech world.

## Website Features

The website includes the following pages:

1. **Home Page**: Introduces the institute, its mission, and key programs
2. **About Page**: Provides detailed information about the institute's history, mission, team, and impact
3. **Donate Page**: Allows supporters to make donations to support the institute's work
4. **Programs Page**: Details the educational pathways offered by the institute
5. **Volunteer Page**: Information for those interested in volunteering their time and skills
6. **Contact Us Page**: Multiple forms for different types of inquiries and contact information

## Technology Stack

- HTML5
- CSS3
- JavaScript
- Font Awesome icons
- Google Fonts

## Educational Pathway

The institute offers a comprehensive educational pathway:

1. **Digital Literacy** (3 months): Foundation in computer basics, internet skills, and office applications
2. **Tech Specialization** (6 months): Advanced training in areas like Graphic Design, UX/UI, Frontend/Backend Development, Data Science, Data Analytics, and Animation
3. **Job Placement**: Connecting graduates with employment and internship opportunities
4. **University Pathway** (8 months): Preparation for higher education through partner universities

## Sustainable Technology

The institute uses solar power to ensure reliable access to technology even in areas with limited electricity.
